Our Services

Comprehensive student accommodation solutions from development to management

What We Offer

Zero 16 Enterprise provides comprehensive student accommodation solutions in Randfontein. From development and construction to daily operations and tenant management, we handle every aspect of creating safe, modern living spaces for students. Our purpose-built residences near Westcol TVET College feature high-speed internet, study areas, recreational facilities, and 24/7 security.

Our Service Portfolio

With expertise in property development, facilities management, and real estate consulting, we deliver end-to-end solutions for student accommodation. Our services are designed to meet NSFAS standards while providing students with affordable, quality living environments that support their academic journey. Each service is tailored to create sustainable, well-managed properties that benefit students, investors, and communities.

Student Accommodation Development

We design and build purpose-built student residences with modern amenities, study spaces, and recreational facilities. Our developments are strategically located near educational institutions to provide convenient, quality living spaces.

Property Management & Daily Operations

Comprehensive management of student accommodation properties including maintenance, tenant relations, rent collection, and operational oversight. We ensure your property runs smoothly and efficiently every day.

Facilities Management

Professional maintenance and upkeep of all facilities including security systems, internet infrastructure, study areas, recreational spaces, and common areas to ensure optimal living conditions for students.

Leasing & Tenant Management

Expert handling of lease agreements, student applications, move-ins and move-outs, and ongoing tenant support. We ensure smooth processes that comply with NSFAS requirements and institutional standards.

Real Estate Consulting

Strategic advisory services for student accommodation investments, market analysis, feasibility studies, and development planning. We help investors and institutions make informed decisions in the student housing sector.

DFBOT Projects

Design, Finance, Build, Operate, and Transfer projects for student accommodation. We deliver complete turnkey solutions from concept to handover, managing every phase of development and operation.
